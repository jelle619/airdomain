How does it work?
  1. Create your site. Make it look good and functional. Your site must use modern web standards and be well optimised for different devices.
  2. Make your site open source. Open source doesn't neccesairely have to mean that it's free to use. You will still be able to keep your copyright.
  3. Choose your domain. The reason why we're able to offer these domain names for free, is because they're actually really short subdomains (e.g. yourdomain.dp.us).
How can I link my website with it?

There are three ways to link your site: redirect, hosted and iframe.

Redirect: Users who visit your domain will be redirected to another URL where your site is hosted.

Hosted: We will host your site's files. Visitors who visit your domain wll stay on that domain and be able to view the website.

Iframe (not recommended): Visitors who visit your domain won't be redirected. They'll get to see a fullscreen iframe connected to your site's adress.
